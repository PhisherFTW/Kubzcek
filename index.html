Focus Areas

  • Do not profile check at the beginning of games as this is a mental block that a lot of players face, if they see banned on other games or 5k+ hours they get intimidated or see in comments -rep cheater.
  • Do not run through mollies on the group longer than 1.5 seconds.
  • Attempt to play within the optimal range of whatever weapon you are using during the previous game this was most noticeable with your p250 taking a lot of fights outside of the optimal range.
  • Over Aggression, this was demonstrated a few times leading to you throwing away your life early without being able to have an impact later on in the round and assist your teammates.
  • Whenever you do movement do it with full focus and make sure the enemy cannot shoot you while you are jumping otherwise you are defenseless and can’t shoot back accurately.

FFA Community-Deathmatch Notes

  • Overflicking hugely when startled causing you to have to do huge adjustments even at close range.
  • Spray is loose and not super controlled
  • Long-distance engagements are quite inaccurate because of high sens.
  • Tracking is quite shaky.
